Noon Sakinah (نْ) & Tanween (ــًــٍــٌ)

Izhar, Idgham (with & without Ghunnah), Iqlab, Ikhfa.

Rules Overview

Noon Sakinah and Tanween have 4 main rules:

Izhar (إظهار) — Clear Idgham with Ghunnah: ي ن م و Idgham without Ghunnah: ل ر Iqlab (إقلاب) — to Meem Ikhfa (إخفاء) — Concealment
